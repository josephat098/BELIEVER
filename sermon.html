Trusting God in Difficult Times

In this message, Pastor John explores how we can maintain our faith and trust in God even when facing life's greatest challenges.

Drawing from the story of Job and Paul's letters, we learn practical steps to strengthen our faith during trials.

Scripture Focus: Romans 8:28 - "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."
